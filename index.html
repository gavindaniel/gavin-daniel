About Me

LinkedIn GitHub

Hey, I'm Gavin.

I'm an engineer with an eye for improving things.

For the last three years I was helping program and implement water and wastewater control systems for cities and utility districts around Washington state.

Before starting my professional career, I studied Electrical & Computer Engineering at the University of Arizona. My focus was in object-oriented programming and software development.

In my senior year I was a member of the New Venture Development program in which I designed an application for improving the brick & mortar shopping experience.

While I was getting my undergraduate degree, I worked as an I.T. assistant for U.S. Geological Survey and a lab assistant for a class in computer programming for engineering applications.

When I'm not working, I'm either out taking pictures, working on an art project, watching a baseball game or at a music concert with my friends.
